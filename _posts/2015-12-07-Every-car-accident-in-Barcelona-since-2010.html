Every Barcelona car accident from 2010-2014

Zoom in and around, and click on the markers for details about each accident.

Data sources, code, etc.

OpenDataBCN hosts the dataset for traffic accidents managed by the Guardia Urbana. It's a super interesting dataset with a lot more than what was explored here (mortality, number of injuries, etc.).

The code and data for this post (including source files, cleaning script, visualizations, etc.) can all be found on github.
